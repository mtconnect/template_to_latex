
h1. @Relationships@

* Part : Part 2

h2. Parent: @Structural Elements for MTConnectDevices@
 
@Relationships@ is an XML container that organizes information defining the affiliation between pieces of equipment that function independently but together perform a manufacturing operation.  @Relationship@ may also define the affiliation between components within a piece of equipment.
 
@Relationships@ may be modeled as part of a @Device@ or a @Component@ _Structural Element_. 

@Relationships@ contains one or more @Relationship@ XML elements.
